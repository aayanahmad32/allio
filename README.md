ALLIO PRO is the ultimate global media downloader that allows you to download videos and music from all social media platforms including Sora 2 AI, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter, Telegram, Terabox, StreamNet, DiskWala, and more.

## Features

- **Sora 2 AI Support**: Download and extract prompts from OpenAI's Sora 2 AI videos
- **8K Video Downloads**: Download videos in stunning 8K resolution
- **Hi-Res Audio Extraction**: Extract audio in high-quality MP3, M4A, or FLAC formats
- **Embedded Lyrics**: Automatically find and embed lyrics into your MP3 files
- **Multi-Platform Support**: Download from 20+ social media platforms
- **Lightning Fast**: Advanced technology ensures fast downloads
- **No Registration Required**: Start downloading immediately without creating an account
- **Completely Free**: No hidden charges or subscription fees

## Getting Started

1. Visit [ALLIO PRO](https://alliopro.com)
2. Paste the URL of the video or music you want to download
3. Select your preferred format and quality
4. Click the download button

## Installation

ALLIO PRO is a web-based application and doesn't require installation. Simply visit our website to start downloading.
